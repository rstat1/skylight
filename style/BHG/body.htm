Universe Alliance


The year is 2184. The human race has ended world wars by forming a world government and an alliance with many of the alien races. The super race known as the K'yaar has been planning to dominate the universe by piecing together an ancient relic that will give them supreme power. The humans have become a threat to their mission and then the war began.
